# UC Brand Guidelines

## [The wordmark](uc-wordmark-blue-official.png)

The official UC wordmark is the approved relationship between the words “University,” “of,” and “California”. 

<img src="uc-wordmark-blue-official.png" />

### [Wordmark variation](uc-wordmark-official.png)

- [Kievit regular](/fonts/KievitWebPro-Book.woff)
- [Kievit bold](/fonts/KievitWebPro-Bold)

### Secondary Typeface (serif): Lyon

- [Lyon regular](/fonts/lyontext-regular-web.woff)
- [Lyon bold](/fonts/lyontext-bold-web.woff)

### Alternative Typeface (When Kievit & Lyon Unavailable)

- Sans-serif: Ariel
- Serif: Georgia
