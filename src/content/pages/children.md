# LII — Life Impact International

AP0110 is partnered with **Life Impact International (LII)** — Reach. Rescue. Restore. — protecting vulnerable and exploited children across Thailand, Myanmar, and Brazil.

## Our Impact

- 1,000+ children served daily
- 122 field staff and partners
- 4 countries of operation

## Independent Internet for Every Child

AP0110 equips LII with Web 4.0 mesh networking — resilient, low-cost, field-deployable connectivity that operates without cloud dependency or centralized infrastructure:

- Eliminate reliance on expensive cloud services.
- Bypass faulty local grid infrastructure.
- Enable encrypted communications in restricted environments.

## Technology Deployment Matrix

Local and international laws restrict the import and use of certain technologies in each operating country. AP0110 maps which technologies (cloud, VPN, Wi-Fi/LAN, LoRaWAN, SDR, smart cameras, weather stations, solar + battery, handheld radios) are permitted across Thailand, Myanmar, and Brazil, and deploys accordingly.
