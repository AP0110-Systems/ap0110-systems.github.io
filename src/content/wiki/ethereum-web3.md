## Summary

The Ethereum Foundation's explainer frames Web3 as an internet owned by its builders and users rather than by platforms — decentralized, verifiable, and built on programmable smart contracts. It generalizes [Nakamoto](/wiki/nakamoto/)'s ledger from money to arbitrary applications and ownership.

This is a *living* web page maintained by its publisher; only an original summary and the canonical link are kept here.

## Why it matters

Web3 added programmable ownership to the [decentralization](/wiki/decentralization/) story. The Independent Internet (Web 4.0) builds on it by adding *control* of the underlying compute and data — self-hosting, mesh networking, and post-quantum security on top of ownership.
