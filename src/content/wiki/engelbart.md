**Douglas Engelbart** (1925–2013) led the Augmentation Research Center at SRI, where he developed the mouse, hypertext editing, and collaborative tools — showcased in the 1968 "Mother of All Demos."

## Key work in the library

- [Augmenting Human Intellect](/docs/augmenting-human-intellect/) (1962) — a conceptual framework for raising human problem-solving capability with computers.

## Significance

Engelbart turned [Licklider](/wiki/licklider/)'s symbiosis into engineering, building directly on [Vannevar Bush](/wiki/vannevar-bush/)'s Memex. His goal of amplifying *ordinary* people's capability — not concentrating it — directly informs the equity principle of the Independent Internet.
