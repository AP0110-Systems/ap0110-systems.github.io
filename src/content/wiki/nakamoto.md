**Satoshi Nakamoto** is the pseudonym of the person or group who designed Bitcoin and released its reference implementation in 2008–2009. Their identity remains unknown.

## Key works in the library

- [the Bitcoin whitepaper](/docs/bitcoin-whitepaper/) (2008) — peer-to-peer electronic cash secured by proof-of-work.
- Generalized for applications by [Ethereum's Web3](/docs/ethereum-web3/).

## Significance

Nakamoto delivered the strongest practical proof of [decentralization](/wiki/decentralization/): a globally shared ledger with no central owner. This is the "Own" axis the Independent Internet builds on by adding *control*.
